OUR STORY

We Build Builders.

Code & Circuits was founded on a single belief: that the gap between understanding technology and building with it should not exist. We bridge theory and practice through radical hands-on education.

MISSION

Democratising STEM Education

The world's most important technologies — IoT, artificial intelligence, robotics — are built by engineers. Yet access to quality, practical engineering education remains unequal. We are changing that.

Code & Circuits is a remote-first platform that delivers university-grade STEM education at a fraction of the cost, with the practical depth that traditional institutions often miss.
